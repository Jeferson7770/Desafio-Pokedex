You may use project names in factual references, such as:

- discussing compatibility
- linking to the original project
- describing upstream origin

## Restricted Uses

You may not, without written permission:

- use Fince logos or brand assets in your product identity
- imply endorsement, partnership, or official status
- use confusingly similar names for forks or services
- present modified versions as official Fince releases

## Forks and Derivatives

Forks are allowed under AGPL terms, but must:

- use distinct branding
- clearly state they are unofficial
- avoid visual or naming confusion with official Fince offerings
